[Privacy policy.docx](https://github.com/user-attachments/files/25852441/Privacy.policy.docx) Information the App May Access To deliver its features, Glocal may access the following device capabilities and information: Microphone Used to record voice reminders and support speech-based reminder creation. Location (optional) Used to obtain local weather information and support optional location-aware profiles. Notifications and Alarms Used to deliver reminders, hourly announcements, and scheduled notifications. Calendar (optional) If enabled by the user, Glocal may access calendar events to announce upcoming events. App Settings and Reminder Content This includes preferences such as language selection, voice choice, quiet hours, reminder text or audio, and related configuration settings. How Information Is Used Information accessed by Glocal is used solely to provide the app’s core functionality, including: Hourly time announcements Hourly weather announcements Creation and playback of reminders Voice-based reminder setup Language and voice personalisation Offline operation using cached weather information The app is designed to operate primarily on-device. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services Glocal does not sell personal data. To retrieve weather information, the app may send approximate location coordinates to a third-party weather service provider. This information is used only to return weather data for the user’s location. Glocal does not use reminder content, voice recordings, or personal information for targeted advertising. Except for weather data requests required for functionality, Glocal does not share personal data with external parties. Data Storage and Security Most data used by Glocal is stored locally on the user’s device. This includes: reminders language preferences selected voices quiet hours and notification settings Where supported by the device operating system, secure storage and encryption mechanisms are used to protect sensitive information. Weather responses may be cached locally to support low-bandwidth or offline usage. App Permissions Glocal requests only permissions necessary to operate its core features: Microphone Used for recording voice reminders and speech input. Location Used to obtain local weather information and optional location-based profiles. Notifications Used to deliver reminders and scheduled announcements. Calendar (optional) Used to announce upcoming events if the feature is enabled. Users may revoke these permissions at any time through their device settings. Data Retention and Deletion Reminder data, settings, and preferences remain on the device until the user edits or deletes them. Users may delete: reminders voice recordings saved locations Uninstalling the application removes locally stored data, subject to the device operating system’s standard behaviour. Children’s Privacy Glocal is not specifically directed toward children. The application does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If it becomes known that personal information from a child has been collected unintentionally, appropriate steps will be taken to delete such information. User Rights Depending on the user’s region, including jurisdictions governed by privacy regulations such as General Data Protection Regulation, users may have rights to: access information held about them request correction of inaccurate information request deletion of personal data Requests can be submitted through the contact information below. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
